+++ About Griff

You might be wondering about the name. Well

  - *Griff* is to *Begriffe* what *for* is to *before* and *gin* is
    to *begin*.

Griff is a collection of software packages that support the
application of formal concept analysis to various sources of
information. These packages are in various stages of
development. Sarl3 and CASS are recommended.

Griff is currently hosted on [Sourceforge][http://sf.net/projects/griff/].

Many related papers can be found on the [KVO website][http://kvocentral.org].
